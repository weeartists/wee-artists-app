A note about analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to improve the app experience. No personal information, no children's data, no drawings — ever. You can opt out at any time in app settings under Settings → Privacy → Analytics.

👋 About Wee Artists

Wee Artists is a creative drawing app made for young children to explore imagination in a safe, joyful space. We built it with one principle above everything else: children deserve a private, ad-free place to create.

This Privacy Policy explains clearly what we do — and don't do — with any information related to your use of the app. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation for children (GDPR-K), Apple's App Store Guidelines for Kids Category apps, and the Kids Online Safety Act (KOSA).

🔥 Firebase Analytics — How It Works

We use Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC. It helps us understand how the app is being used in aggregate — like which drawing tools are most popular — so we can make Wee Artists better for every child.

🔥
Google Firebase Analytics

Data is anonymised and aggregated. Firebase does not allow us to identify individual users. All data is reported in aggregate (for example, "500 users opened the stamp tool today").

No advertising IDs are used. We have disabled all advertising-related features in Firebase. No data is used for targeted advertising of any kind.

Child-directed settings enabled. Firebase is configured in child-directed mode as required by COPPA and Google's Families Policy. This disables all personalised advertising, remarketing, and demographic data collection.

Data retention: Analytics event data is retained for 2 months, after which it is automatically deleted by Google.

Google LLC's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy. For further information, parents and guardians may search for "Google Privacy Policy" and "Firebase Privacy Information" on the web.

🚪

You can opt out at any time. Go to Settings → Privacy → Analytics in the app to disable analytics collection entirely. Once disabled, no usage data of any kind is sent from your device.

🧒 Children's Privacy (COPPA, GDPR-K & KOSA)

Wee Artists is specifically built for young children. We fully comply with:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, USA) — We do not collect personal information from any child under 13 without verifiable parental consent. No such consent is needed because we collect no personal information at all.
  • GDPR-K (GDPR provisions for children's data, EU/UK) — All data processing is limited to anonymous, aggregate analytics that cannot identify any child.
  • KOSA (Kids Online Safety Act) — The app does not contain features that promote addictive behaviour, social comparison, harmful content, or unsupervised communication.
  • Apple Kids Category Guidelines — The app contains no advertising, no third-party analytics beyond Firebase (configured for child-directed use), no social features, and no external links accessible to children.

🍎 Apple App Store Compliance

Wee Artists is listed in the Apple App Store Kids Category. In accordance with Apple's requirements: the app contains no behavioural advertising, no third-party data collection for commercial purposes, no links out of the app visible to children, and no ability for children to make purchases without parental approval.

All analytics data is collected in child-directed mode with advertising features disabled, consistent with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines for apps designed for children.

🤝 Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated in Wee Artists is Firebase Analytics (by Google LLC), used solely for anonymous usage analytics as described above. There are no:

  • Advertisements or ad networks of any kind
  • Social media integrations or sharing features
  • In-app purchases linked to external payment services
  • Additional tracking, profiling, or marketing tools
  • External links or websites accessible from within the app
  • Chat, messaging, or user-generated content features

🔐 Data Security

We take the security of children's privacy seriously. Because we collect no personal data, the risk of a data breach affecting your child is minimal by design.

The only data transmitted from the app is anonymous analytics events sent securely to Firebase over encrypted HTTPS connections. This data cannot be used to identify or locate any individual user or child.

All artwork remains on your device under the security protections provided by Apple iOS/iPadOS.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes — such as introducing new data collection — we will notify users via an in-app notice and seek parental consent where required by law (including COPPA and GDPR-K).

We will never introduce advertising, personal data collection, or external communication features without providing clear notice and obtaining appropriate consent.
