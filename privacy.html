👋 About Wee Artists

Wee Artists is a creative drawing app designed for children to explore imagination in a safe, joyful space. We built it with one principle above everything else: children deserve a private, ad-free place to create.

This Privacy Policy explains clearly what we do — and don't do — with any information related to your use of the app. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation for children (GDPR-K), and the Kids Online Safety Act (KOSA).

We collect no personal data from children. No account, login, or registration is required to use Wee Artists. The app contains no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising of any kind.

🧒 Children's Privacy (COPPA, GDPR-K & KOSA)

Wee Artists is designed for children. We fully comply with:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, USA) — We do not collect personal information from any child under 13. No parental consent is needed because we collect no personal information from children.
  • GDPR-K (GDPR provisions for children's data, EU/UK) — We collect no personal data from children, making this the simplest possible compliance: nothing about a child is processed.
  • KOSA (Kids Online Safety Act) — The app does not contain features that promote addictive behaviour, social comparison, harmful content, or unsupervised communication.

🍎 Apple App Store Compliance

Wee Artists is listed in the Apple App Store Education category with a 4+ age rating. In accordance with Apple's requirements: the app contains no behavioural advertising, no analytics or tracking SDKs, no social features accessible to children, and all in-app purchase flows require parental involvement via a parental gate.

💳 In-App Purchases & RevenueCat

Wee Artists offers an optional premium subscription to unlock all drawing lessons. Subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple's App Store — we never receive or store your payment details.

To manage subscription status, the app uses RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service. When you make or restore a purchase, RevenueCat receives your Apple purchase receipt and an anonymous device identifier solely to verify whether a subscription is active.

📋 What RevenueCat receives

Purchase receipt: Sent to RevenueCat's servers to verify your subscription status. This receipt is provided by Apple and contains your subscription details but not your name or payment information.

Anonymous device identifier: Used only to link your subscription to your device. It is not used for advertising or tracking.

No children's personal data is sent to RevenueCat at any time. The purchase flow is protected by a parental gate requiring a parent to complete the transaction.

RevenueCat's full privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

🤝 Third-Party Services

Wee Artists uses only one third-party service: RevenueCat for subscription management (described above). There are no:

  • Analytics or crash reporting tools
  • Advertisements or ad networks of any kind
  • Social media integrations or sharing features
  • Tracking, profiling, or marketing tools
  • External links or websites accessible to children from within the app
  • Chat, messaging, or user-generated content features

🔐 Data Security

We collect no personal data from children and store no artwork on our servers. All drawing content remains on your device under the security protections provided by Apple iOS/iPadOS.

The only data that leaves the device is the Apple purchase receipt sent to RevenueCat when a subscription is purchased or restored — described fully in the In-App Purchases section above.
