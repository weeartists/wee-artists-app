👋 About Wee Artists

Wee Artists is a creative drawing app made for young children to explore imagination in a safe, joyful space. We built it with one principle above everything else: children deserve a private, ad-free place to create.

This Privacy Policy explains clearly what we do — and don't do — with any information related to your use of the app. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation for children (GDPR-K), Apple's App Store Guidelines for Kids Category apps, and the Kids Online Safety Act (KOSA).

🧒 Children's Privacy (COPPA, GDPR-K & KOSA)

Wee Artists is specifically built for young children. We fully comply with:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, USA) — We do not collect personal information from any child under 13. No parental consent is needed because we collect no personal information at all.
  • GDPR-K (GDPR provisions for children's data, EU/UK) — We collect no data of any kind, making this the simplest possible compliance: nothing is processed.
  • KOSA (Kids Online Safety Act) — The app does not contain features that promote addictive behaviour, social comparison, harmful content, or unsupervised communication.
  • Apple Kids Category Guidelines — The app contains no advertising, no analytics or tracking SDKs, no social features, and no external links accessible to children.

🍎 Apple App Store Compliance

Wee Artists is listed in the Apple App Store Kids Category. In accordance with Apple's requirements: the app contains no behavioural advertising, no third-party data collection, no links out of the app visible to children, and no ability for children to make purchases without parental approval.

The app contains no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, or any code that transmits data off the device, consistent with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines for apps designed for children.

🤝 Third-Party Services

Wee Artists uses no third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect or transmit data. There are no:

  • Analytics or crash reporting tools
  • Advertisements or ad networks of any kind
  • Social media integrations or sharing features
  • In-app purchases linked to external payment services
  • Tracking, profiling, or marketing tools
  • External links or websites accessible from within the app
  • Chat, messaging, or user-generated content features

🔐 Data Security

Because we collect no data and the app works entirely offline, there is no data in transit and no server-side storage to secure. The risk of a data breach affecting your child is eliminated by design.

All artwork remains on your device under the security protections provided by Apple iOS/iPadOS.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes — such as introducing new data collection — we will notify users via an in-app notice and seek parental consent where required by law (including COPPA and GDPR-K).

We will never introduce advertising, personal data collection, or external communication features without providing clear notice and obtaining appropriate consent.
