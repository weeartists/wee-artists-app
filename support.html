🛠 Troubleshooting
📵

App won't open

  • Force-quit and relaunch
  • Restart your device
  • Check for updates in the App Store
  • Reinstall if issue continues
🐢

App slow or freezing

  • Close other background apps
  • Check device storage space
  • Restart the device
  • Update to the latest version
✏️

Drawing tools not working

  • Clean the screen gently
  • Try removing screen protector
  • Restart the app
  • Contact us if it persists
💾

Drawings not saving

  • Check available device storage
  • Tap save before closing
  • Restart and try again
  • Email us if unresolved
