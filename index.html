👋 About Wee Artists

Wee Artists is a creative drawing app made for young children to explore imagination in a safe, joyful space. We built it with one principle above everything else: children deserve a private, ad-free place to create.

This Privacy Policy explains clearly what we do — and don't do — with information related to your use of the app.

🔥 Firebase Analytics — How It Works

We use Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC. It helps us understand how the app is being used in aggregate — like which drawing tools are most popular — so we can make Wee Artists better for every child.

🔥
Google Firebase / Analytics

Data is anonymised and aggregated. Firebase does not allow us to identify individual users. All data is reported in aggregate (e.g. "500 users opened the stamp tool today").

No advertising IDs are used. We have disabled all advertising-related features in Firebase. No data is used for targeted advertising of any kind.

Data retention: Analytics event data is retained for 2 months, after which it is automatically deleted by Google.

Firebase is operated by Google LLC. For more details on how Google handles this data, please review the Google Privacy Policy and Firebase Privacy Information.

🚪

You can opt out at any time. Go to Settings → Privacy → Analytics in the app to disable analytics collection entirely. Once disabled, no usage data is sent from your device.

🧒 Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Wee Artists is specifically built for young children. We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and GDPR provisions for children's data protection (GDPR-K).

We do not collect any personal information from children. Firebase Analytics is configured with child-directed settings enabled, which disables all personalised advertising, remarketing, and demographic data collection as required by COPPA and Google's Families Policy.

Analytics data is purely aggregate and anonymous — it is never used to identify, profile, or target individual children in any way. Parents and guardians can turn off analytics entirely in the app's Settings at any time.

🤝 Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated in Wee Artists is Firebase Analytics (by Google LLC), used solely for anonymous usage analytics as described above. There are no:

  • Advertisements or ad networks of any kind
  • Social media integrations or sharing features
  • In-app purchases linked to external payment services
  • Additional tracking, profiling, or marketing tools

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes — such as introducing new data collection — we will notify users via an in-app notice and seek consent where required by law.
